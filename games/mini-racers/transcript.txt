We are tiny cars racing on a track that goes around the kitchen table. We have to do 3 laps and get to the finish line first before the other cars do. The other cars are AI and they bump into us and get in our way. There are also things on the table like cereal boxes and forks and spilled juice that block the track. If we fall off or crash we respawn and keep going. You use a controller plugged into the PC to drive.
